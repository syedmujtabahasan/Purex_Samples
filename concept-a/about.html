Our Story

Purex Chemicals was founded with a single mission: to bring effective, affordable cleaning solutions to every Pakistani household. What began as a small manufacturing unit has grown into a brand trusted across 40+ cities.

Under the leadership of CEO Suleman Bhatti, Purex has built a reputation for no-compromise quality. Every formula is tested, every product is full-strength — because Pakistani families deserve products that actually work.

Today, we serve over 5,000 retailers and 10,000+ homes across Pakistan. From Karachi to Peshawar, from corner stores to supermarkets — Purex is the name shopkeepers and families rely on.
